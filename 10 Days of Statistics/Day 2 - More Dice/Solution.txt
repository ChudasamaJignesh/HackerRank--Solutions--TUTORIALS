Answer: 1/9

There are 6 possibilities on each die.
On 2 dice, there are 6 * 6 = 36 possibilities

There are 4 cases that match the desired criteria:
(1,5) (5,1) (2,4) (4,2)

This gives us a probability of 4/36 = 1/9
